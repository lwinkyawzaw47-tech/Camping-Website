This Privacy Policy explains how Camping Gear ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you visit our website or make a purchase. Please read it carefully.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, as well as data gathered automatically when you use our website.

Personal Information you provide:

  • Full name and email address when you register or contact us
  • Shipping and billing address for order fulfillment
  • Payment details (processed securely — we never store full card numbers)
  • Phone number, if provided voluntarily
  • Messages and feedback submitted via our contact form

Information collected automatically:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited, time spent, and click data
  • Device type and operating system
  • Referring URLs and search terms used to reach us

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to provide, improve, and personalize our services:

  • Process and fulfill your orders, and send order confirmations
  • Respond to your inquiries and customer support requests
  • Send transactional emails such as shipping updates
  • Send promotional offers and newsletters (only with your consent)
  • Analyze website usage to improve our store experience
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud or security issues
  • Comply with legal obligations
We will never sell your personal data to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist us with payment processing, order shipping, email delivery, and website analytics.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction.
  • Your Consent: In any other situation with your explicit permission.

All third-party partners are bound by data protection agreements and are not permitted to use your data for any other purpose.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience.

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core site functions such as your shopping cart and login session.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our pages (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads and track campaign performance.

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality. By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described here.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and employ industry-standard measures to protect it:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmitted to and from our site
  • Secure payment processing via PCI-DSS compliant providers
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls that restrict employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
While we implement robust security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and keep your account credentials confidential.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the link in any email we send.
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Restriction: Request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@campinggear.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites, social media platforms, or partner stores. These sites are not operated by us and have their own privacy policies.

We have no control over, and assume no responsibility for, the content, privacy practices, or policies of any third-party sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of every site you visit.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at hello@campinggear.com and we will take steps to remove that information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

For significant changes, we will notify registered users via email. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Your continued use of our website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Have Privacy Questions?

Our team is happy to help with any questions or concerns about your privacy and data.
